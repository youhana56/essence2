Our Story

Founded in 2010, Essence began as a small perfumery in Grasse, France - the perfume capital of the world. Our founder, Marie Laurent, a third-generation perfumer, wanted to create fragrances that told stories and evoked emotions.

Today, we continue this tradition by sourcing the finest ingredients from around the globe and crafting each perfume with meticulous attention to detail. Our commitment to quality and sustainability remains at the heart of everything we do.
